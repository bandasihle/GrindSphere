About GrindSphere

GrindSphere is a platform designed exclusively for university student, helping them connect with affordable services offered by fellow student

Our mission

We aim to empower student by providing a space where they can show case their skills ,earn income, and access quality services within the university community

How it works

Students can sign up as either **Hustler** (service providers) or **Customers** (those looking for services). Hustlers create profiles. upload porfoilios, and set their prices, while Customers browse and hire based on their needs.

Why choose us?

  • Affordable services: Get quality services at student-friendly prices.
  • Support student hustles : Help your peers grow their business.
  • Exclusive to students: Ensures trust and a safe environment.
